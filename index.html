Workshop "Data Structures in Bioinformatics" 2017

CWI Amsterdam, The Netherlands, February 21-22, 2017

The workshop DSB "Data Structures in Bioinformatics" (DSB) is an incubator of ideas and facilitates exchanges as well as collaborations on topics related to data structures in bioinformatics. Speakers will present state of the art techniques and recent advances in this field.
